## What You're Dealing With

A customer fills out the form on your website. Then someone copies the details into a spreadsheet, types them again into the invoicing tool, and adds the appointment to a calendar by hand. Every step is a chance for a typo, a missed follow-up, or a lead that never gets a reply. The tools could talk to each other. Nobody has ever connected them.

## What You Get

Your tools connected so information flows on its own. Form submissions land in the right place, bookings appear on the right calendars, payments match up with invoices, and follow-ups go out without anyone having to remember. It is all built on the tools you already use and pay for, and documented so you are never locked in.

## What We Take Care Of

- **Forms and leads**: enquiries flow straight into your spreadsheet, inbox, or CRM
- **Booking and calendars**: appointments schedule themselves and stay in sync for everyone involved
- **Payments and invoicing**: tools like Stripe, Square, and QuickBooks connected so records match without retyping
- **Notifications**: the right person finds out right away when something needs attention
- **Reliability**: automations that tell us when they fail instead of failing silently
