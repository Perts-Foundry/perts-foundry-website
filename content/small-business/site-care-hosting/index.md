## What You're Dealing With

The website was fine when it launched, but nobody has touched it since. The hours are wrong, the contact form quietly stopped working, and a customer just mentioned a security warning. You have a hosting bill, a domain registrar, and a handful of subscriptions, and you are not sure what any of them do or who to call when something breaks.

## What You Get

One point of contact for everything that keeps your website healthy. We host it, back it up, watch it, and keep the software underneath it current. When you need something changed, you send us a note instead of logging into anything. When something breaks, it is our problem before it becomes yours.

## What We Take Care Of

- **Hosting**: your site running on fast, reliable infrastructure that we manage
- **Backups**: regular copies of your site kept safely off the server, so nothing is ever truly lost
- **Monitoring**: automated checks that tell us when the site is down or misbehaving
- **Updates**: the software behind the site kept current, so security holes get closed
- **Small changes**: hours, photos, wording, and announcements, handled when you ask
- **Renewals**: domains and certificates renewed on time, in your name
