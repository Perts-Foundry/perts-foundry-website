## What You're Dealing With

Customers look you up before they call, and right now what they find is not helping. Maybe your site looks a decade old, does not work on phones, or was built by someone who is no longer around. Maybe there is no site at all, just a social media page you do not control. Either way, you are losing business to competitors who simply look more put together online.

## What You Get

We design and build a website that presents your business the way you would present it in person: professional, clear, and easy to find. It loads fast, works on every screen size, and tells customers what you do, why you are good at it, and how to reach you. When it launches, everything belongs to you.

## What We Take Care Of

- **Design**: a clean, professional look that fits your business, not a template that fits everyone
- **Writing**: we draft the words with you, so the site sounds like you and not like marketing filler
- **Setup**: domain, hosting, contact forms, and the technical details you should not have to think about
- **The basics done right**: fast loading, mobile friendly, accessible, and set up so search engines can find you
- **Handover**: all accounts and credentials in your name, with plain-English documentation

## How It Works

{{< steps >}}

1. **Talk**: we learn about your business, your customers, and what you want the site to do
2. **Draft**: we design the site and write the first version of the content, then walk through it with you
3. **Refine**: you tell us what to change, and we adjust until it feels right
4. **Launch**: we take the site live, connect your domain, and hand you the keys

{{< /steps >}}

**Who you'll work with:** You deal directly with Seth, the founder, start to finish. Not a call center, not a rotating cast of contractors, just one person who learns your business and stays accountable for the work.
