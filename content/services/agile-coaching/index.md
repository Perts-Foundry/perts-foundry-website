## The Problem

Your team says they're doing agile, but it doesn't feel like it. Standups are status reports. Sprint planning is guesswork. Retrospectives happen but nothing changes. Or maybe you're mid-transition from waterfall and the new process feels like overhead instead of progress. Either way, the goal of shipping faster and more predictably isn't materializing.

## The Outcome

We help your team adopt agile practices that actually improve how they work. Not textbook Scrum imposed from the outside, but practical process improvements tailored to your team's constraints. We've guided teams through waterfall-to-agile transitions that became the model for entire organizations, and shifted teams from Scrum to Kanban when the methodology wasn't matching the work, resulting in a 25% reduction in issue open time.

## Technologies

- **Jira** — workflow design, Kanban board configuration, issue collectors for intake automation, and sprint/backlog management
- **Confluence** — documentation structure, runbooks, and knowledge management to support team processes

## What an Engagement Looks Like

{{< steps >}}

1. **Process Assessment** — We observe your team's current ceremonies, workflows, and pain points to understand what's working and what's not
2. **Methodology Fit** — We recommend the right approach for your team's work, whether that's Scrum, Kanban, or a hybrid, based on how work actually flows
3. **Ceremony Coaching** — We facilitate sprint planning, standups, retrospectives, and demos alongside your team until the patterns become natural
4. **Workflow Optimization** — We configure your tools to match your process, set up intake automation, and establish metrics so your team can see the improvement
