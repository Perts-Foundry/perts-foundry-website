## The Problem

Deploys are scary. They happen at off-hours, require a specific person, and regularly break things. Your team ships less often than they should because the cost of each release is too high.

## The Outcome

Push-button deployments that any engineer on your team can trigger with confidence. Automated testing, progressive rollouts, and instant rollbacks. Ship ten times a day instead of once a week.

## Technologies

- **GitHub Actions / GitLab CI / CircleCI** — we build on whatever your team already uses
- **ArgoCD / Flux** for GitOps-style continuous delivery
- **Docker** for consistent, reproducible build environments
- **Helm / Kustomize** for Kubernetes deployment management
- **Terraform Cloud / Atlantis** for infrastructure change automation

## What an Engagement Looks Like

1. **Pipeline Audit** — We map your current build and deploy flow end-to-end
2. **Quick Wins** — Parallelization, caching, and low-hanging optimizations
3. **Pipeline Build** — Full CI/CD with automated testing gates and deployment strategies
4. **Observability** — Deploy tracking, failure alerts, and rollback automation
