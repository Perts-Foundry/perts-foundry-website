## The Problem

Your team has heard the pitch. Maybe you bought Copilot licenses six months ago and adoption plateaued at a handful of power users. Maybe individual engineers are experimenting with Claude or Cursor on their own, but there is no organizational strategy for what tools to adopt, how to secure them, or how to measure whether they are working. The gap between "we should be using AI" and "AI is making us measurably faster" is where most engineering organizations get stuck.

The risks compound quietly. Without guidance, engineers paste proprietary code into public-facing tools. Without evaluation, you pay for overlapping licenses that solve the same problem. Without integration into existing workflows, AI tools become shelfware: technically available, practically unused. And without someone who has rolled this out before, every team reinvents the wheel independently.

## The Outcome

We take AI tooling from experiment to operational capability. Your team gets the right tools selected for their actual workflow, enterprise licensing evaluated and secured, and AI-powered code review integrated directly into your CI/CD pipelines. Engineers build proficiency through hands-on pairing, not slide decks. The result is a team that ships faster with higher quality, backed by an internal best-practices guide they can maintain after we leave.

We bring direct experience from driving AI adoption across a large engineering organization: evaluating and licensing Claude Enterprise, Cursor, and CodeRabbit; integrating AI code review across 30+ repositories; coaching over 20 engineers through hands-on sessions; and delivering technical presentations that catalyzed organization-wide tooling evaluation.

## Technologies

- **Claude & Claude Code** -- complex reasoning tasks including architecture review, large-scale refactoring, and specialized AI agent development, with experience building production tooling via the Claude API and Anthropic Python SDK
- **Cursor** -- AI-assisted development for infrastructure and application code, used extensively for large-scale Terraform and Snowflake migrations across multiple cloud providers
- **GitHub Copilot** -- in-IDE code completion evaluated and licensed for enterprise-wide rollout alongside other AI tooling
- **CodeRabbit** -- AI-powered code review integrated across 30+ repositories in CI/CD pipelines, automatically surfacing security vulnerabilities and code quality issues in every pull request
