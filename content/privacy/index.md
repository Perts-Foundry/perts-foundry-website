## Overview

Perts Foundry respects your privacy. This site is designed to collect as little data as possible while still providing a useful experience.

## What We Collect

**Website Analytics:** This site uses [Cloudflare Web Analytics](https://www.cloudflare.com/web-analytics/), a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies, does not track individual users, and does not collect personal information. Analytics data is aggregated and cannot be tied back to any individual visitor.

**Contact Information:** If you contact us via email, we retain your message and contact details solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to mailing lists or share your information with third parties.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not use cookies or local storage for tracking
- We do not use third-party advertising or retargeting
- We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties
- We do not use fingerprinting or other cross-site tracking techniques

## Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on [Cloudflare Workers](https://workers.cloudflare.com/). Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal operations. These logs are governed by [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
